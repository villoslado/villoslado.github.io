Assorted 10: A Partnership Portfolio

Amazon's Kindle Fire sets Bing as default search engine

Microsoft's Bing became the default search engine on the Amazon device web browser. This was part of Microsoft's strategy to catch Google in search market share. In the early days, this was a $100M+ business for Microsoft.

Replacing Google as the default search engine on Apple's Safari

In 2015-16, we estimated Google was paying $4B to Apple for being the default search engine. We decided to engage in a bidding war to replace it with Bing as we were focused on enhancing our product.

News Article

Building a robust partner ecosystem for Microsoft Teams

Partners played a critical role in helping Microsoft customers use Teams to streamline processes. Contact center solutions and call recording for compliance played a key role to enable customer-focused and regulated-industry players.

Blog Post

Cisco Webex Video Integration for Microsoft Teams

Modern workplaces have a mix of different vendors across their collaboration and productivity portfolios, making interoperability and integrations critical to enable work productivity. Microsoft customers requested this interoperability to be a reality.

Press Release

Zoom, Microsoft, the early days

In late 2017, Microsoft strategists proposed acquiring Zoom, but top execs rejected it, favoring the development of Teams. I advised and supported this decision, recognizing the potential of building an ecosystem for Teams, which later included Zoom.

News Article Blog Post

Five9, as a potential M&A target

In 2018, Microsoft considered my proposal to acquire Five9 as many of our customers were looking at Teams for critical workflow integrations. Ultimately, we focused on enhancing our first-party capabilities. Later, in 2021, Zoom attempted to acquire Five9.

Press Release

Revitalizing the Oracle-Microsoft partnership

I successfully reset the strained relationship between Oracle and Microsoft, a challenge previously unmet. This turnaround fostered new collaborative discussions and a productive alliance, paving the way for joint innovation.

Blog Post

Cerner turns health data into actionable insights with Tableau

Cerner Corporation was an early and invaluable Tableau OEM partner. They initially embedded Tableau Server into their offerings, specifically their cloud-based platform HealtheIntent, as the primary method for showing value-add visualizations.

Blog Post

Cart.com embeds Tableau in Snowflake, powering their platform

Cart.com used Snowflake and Tableau to power their end-to-end e-commerce platform. Their goal was to empower their business customers to make insightful decisions in their flow of work. They were an invaluable partner who pushed us to innovate.

Blog Post

Kantata embeds Tableau capabilities into its SX suite

Kantata, one of the largest ISVs in the Salesforce ecosystem, utilized Tableau functionality to create Kantata SX Embedded Analytics, enabling customers to interactively slice, dice, and report on key business performance metrics.
